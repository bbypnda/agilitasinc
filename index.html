The idea behind Faithful Depictions

Faithful Depictions was created from a desire to make Christian artwork that feels warm, peaceful, emotionally meaningful, and welcoming in a variety of homes and spaces.

The collection focuses on broadly Christian themes rather than denomination-specific imagery. Its purpose is to encourage reflection, remind viewers of Christ's love, and create moments of comfort and hope through visual art.

Technology may assist in the creative process, but each product is selected, reviewed, prepared, and presented with care before it is made available to customers.

“Created to bring peace, hope, comfort, and faith-filled beauty into everyday life.”
